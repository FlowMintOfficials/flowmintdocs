FAQ

  • How do I pay for Pro?

    Choose a plan above, then send the plan amount via UPI to our UPI ID using any UPI app (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.). After payment, click “Confirm Payment via email” and attach a screenshot of the payment. We’ll verify and send your license key to your email.

  • When will I receive my license key?

    Your license will be issued within 12–24 hours after we verify your payment. We manually check each payment and then email the license key to the address you used when you sent the “Payment done” email.

  • Why is verification manual?

    We manually verify each payment and screenshot to prevent fraud and ensure the correct plan and amount. This also help us keep pricing affordable by avoiding payment gateway fees.

  • What if I don’t receive the license key?

    Check your spam folder first. If you still don’t see it after 24 hours, reply to your original “Payment confirmation” email or contact us at the support email with your payment details and we’ll help you.

  • Can I switch or cancel my plan?

    You can choose a different plan when your current license expires. For refunds or plan changes, contact us with your email and order details and we’ll assist you.

  • Is my payment and data safe?

    Yes. Payment is done directly through your UPI app (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.) — we never see or store your card, UPI PIN, or bank details. We only receive your email and payment screenshot for verification. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Your information is used only to verify payment and send your license key.
